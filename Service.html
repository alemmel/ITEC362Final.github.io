Services

Our services are available 24/7.

Carolina Sound employs factory-trained technicians; ensuring fast, quality installations. Service call responses come within 24 hours.

Carolina Sound offers Emergency Service. If your system has to be fixed immediately, Emergency Service will send technicians within 4 hours anywhere in the state. Any time - nights, weekends, holidays - it doesn't matter. You're protected.

Carolina Sound offers Maintenance Contracts, a great way to cap your maintenance expenses to a fixed monthly amount. Contracts are available on all systems and are billed at a flat monthly rate which can cover labor, parts, or both. Loaner equipment is available for most systems in case factory repairs are required. Contract rates vary depending on your system and the level of service that you need. Contact your Carolina Sound representative today for a free custom quote.

Our installers are trained and experienced in helping you get the most from your system. Every piece of your system is installed with three main concerns: Long-term reliability, excellent ergonomics, and a positive aesthetic appearance.

All of our design engineers hold 4-year engineering degrees and so do the majority of our project engineers. Carolina Sound will design your system and issue you written specifications and/or CAD drawings to allow you to install your own system or put a design out for bid.

Carolina Sound will design your system and see it through to perfect implementation. Our engineers take the time to sit down with you and your staff to deeply explore your communications goals. We don't just want to know what kind of system you need - we want to know how you want to use it.

Once your system is expertly designed, installed, and tested, Carolina Sound offers preventive maintenance plans that will keep your systems functioning like new for as long as you need them.
