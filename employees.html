CEO
Charles G. Perry IV

Design and project supervision is provided by Charles G. Perry IV. Mr. Perry holds an electrical engineering degree from the University of Virginia’s School of Engineering and Applied Science. An audio hobbyist for more than fifteen years, Mr. Perry has created award winning audio systems for every type of venue, from department stores to high-performance automobiles. Mr. Perry is a factory trained programmer for Crestron control systems, Biamp Digital Signal Processors and many other similar systems. He is a Crestron-certified DigitalMedia Engineer (DMC-E), enabling him to create state-of-the-art digital video distribution systems.

Sales Representative
David Reis Perry

Sales is managed by David Perry, a graduate of Emory University. David has graduated from Leadership Charleston with the Charleston-Trident Chamber of Commerce.

Operations Manager
Deborah Bunting

Deborah Bunting has worked with Carolina Sound for over 18 years. As our Operations Manager, she handles the day-to-day workings of all three Carolina Sound and Georgia Sound offices, coordinates the schedules of our installation and service technicians and keeps our teams running smoothly. Deborah is the person you're most likely to hear at our end of the phone and is the right person to efficiently take care of any needs or concerns you may have.
